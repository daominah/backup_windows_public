## Fallback Versions for GTX 1060

If the newest driver lags on an old GTX 1060, try one of the following:

- v537.58: <https://www.nvidia.com/download/driverResults.aspx/212701/en-us/>
- v551.86: <https://www.nvidia.com/download/driverResults.aspx/223499/en-us/>
