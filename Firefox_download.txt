## Explain installer types:

* win64 exe: normal Windows installer (probably you want this)
* win64 msi: Windows installer for system administrators
* win64 aarch64: for CPU with ARM architecture
* EME-free: without Encrypted Media Extensions, for people who oppose services like Netflix, Spotify, ...
* version with "b": e.g. 133.0b9 is a beta version, older than 133.0
