## Installer Types

- `win64 exe`: normal Windows installer (probably what you want)
- `win64 msi`: Windows installer for system administrators
- `win64 aarch64`: for CPUs with ARM architecture
- `EME-free`: without Encrypted Media Extensions, for people who oppose services like Netflix, Spotify, etc.
- version with "b": e.g. `133.0b9` is a beta version, older than `133.0`
